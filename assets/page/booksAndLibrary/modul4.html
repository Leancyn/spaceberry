Module 4: Agreement, Suggestion, and Uncertainty

A. Objectives

  • Understand various expressions of agreement both for positive and negative expressions
  • Understand various expressions of suggestions and uncertainty

B. Material Description

4.a. Agreement

Agreement digunakan untuk menyatakan bahwa kita setuju dengan orang lain. Bisa dalam bentuk positif (jika setuju dengan kalimat positif), atau negatif (jika setuju dengan kalimat negatif).

  • Positive Agreement: So do I, Me too, I agree, You can say that again
  • Negative Agreement: Neither do I, I don’t either

Example 1:

Man: This should be a two-semester course. One semester is just not enough.
Woman: You can say that again!
Answer: B. She agrees strongly with the man.

Example 2:

Woman: I don’t like rainy days.
Man: I don’t either.
Answer: A. He also dislikes rainy days.

Exercise 4.1

Underline the agreement expressions in each dialogue and choose the correct interpretation.

4.b. Suggestion

Ekspresi suggestion (saran) biasanya menggunakan frasa seperti: Let’s..., Why don’t we..., How about..., What if..., Shall we...?

Example 1:

Man: We need to discuss our presentation.
Woman: How about over lunch today?
Answer: B. During lunch.

Example 2:

Woman: We haven’t done any practice yet.
Man: Why don’t we meet tomorrow and rehearse?
Answer: A. He’s suggesting a meeting for practice.

Exercise 4.2

Identify and underline the suggestion expressions and choose the most suitable meaning.

4.c. Uncertainty

Ketika seseorang tidak yakin atau belum pasti tentang sesuatu, mereka biasanya menggunakan ekspresi seperti: I think, I guess, As far as I know, Maybe, Probably, It seems.

Example 1:

Woman: Is the bill due on the first or the fifteenth?
Man: On the fifteenth, as far as I know.
Answer: C. He thinks the bill is due in the middle of the month.

Example 2:

Man: Will Sarah be at the meeting?
Woman: I think she might be late.
Answer: B. She may not arrive on time.

Exercise 4.3

Underline expressions of uncertainty in the conversation and choose the best interpretation.

C. Review Exercises

Identify expressions of agreement, suggestion, and uncertainty in the conversations and choose the correct answer.

Review Example:

Man: I don’t think we’ll finish on time.
Woman: Neither do I.
Narrator: What does the woman mean?
Answer: A. She agrees with the man’s opinion.

D. Summary Table

Category Common Expressions Function
Positive Agreement So do I, Me too, You can say that again Agreeing with positive statements
Negative Agreement Neither do I, I don’t either Agreeing with negative statements
Suggestions Let’s..., How about..., Why don’t we... Giving ideas or proposals
Uncertainty I think..., As far as I know..., Maybe, Probably Expressing unsure or doubtful statements
