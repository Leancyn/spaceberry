Module 7: Agreement

A. Objectives

After studying this material, students are able to:

  • Identify expressions of agreement or disagreement in short conversations
  • Distinguish between agreeing, disagreeing, and uncertain responses

B. Material Description

Agreement refers to expressing support, consent, or a shared opinion with another speaker. These expressions are often found in everyday conversations and listening tasks.

7.a. Positive Agreement

Used when agreeing with a positive statement.

  • "So do I"
  • "I agree"
  • "Exactly"
  • "That’s right"
Woman: I love watching movies in the cinema.
Man: So do I. The atmosphere is different from home.
Answer: C. He also enjoys watching movies in the cinema.

Exercise 7.1

Underline positive agreement expressions and choose the most accurate interpretation.

7.b. Negative Agreement

Used when agreeing with a negative statement.

  • "Neither do I"
  • "I don’t either"
Man: I didn’t enjoy the seminar last week.
Woman: I don’t either. It was quite boring.
Answer: A. She also didn’t enjoy the seminar.

Exercise 7.2

Underline negative agreement expressions and explain their implied meaning.

7.c. Disagreement and Uncertainty

These are used when a speaker does not share the same opinion, or is unsure.

  • Disagreement: "I don’t think so", "Not really", "Actually, no"
  • Uncertainty: "Maybe", "I’m not sure", "It depends"
Woman: He should be promoted. He’s worked really hard.
Man: Actually, no. He hasn’t finished several major tasks.
Answer: B. He disagrees about the promotion.

Exercise 7.3

Identify disagreement or uncertainty and choose the correct interpretation.

Summary Example:

Man: The math test was extremely difficult.
Woman: I agree. I left most answers blank.
Answer: A. She also found the test difficult.

C. Review Exercises

Identify whether each response in the dialogue shows:

  • A: Agreement
  • B: Disagreement
  • C: Uncertainty

Example Review Item:

Man: I think we’ll win the competition.
Woman: Maybe, but it depends on our final presentation.
Answer: C. Uncertainty

D. Summary Table of Expressions

Expression Type Meaning

Neither do I Negative Agreement I also do not agree
I don’t think so Disagreement I have a different opinion
Actually, no Disagreement Expressing a direct contradiction
I’m not sure Uncertainty Lack of confidence in the answer
That’s right Positive Agreement Confirming something is true
