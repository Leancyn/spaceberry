Module 2: Who, What, Where

A. Objectives

  • Draw a conclusion about: a) people, things, and places; b) people and things in passive sentences
  • Understand the questions and answers which may appear in passive and active voice

B. Material Description

2.a. Questions about People, Things, and Places

Dalam percakapan pendek, kita sering diminta untuk menarik kesimpulan tentang:

  • Who → Siapa yang melakukan atau mengalami sesuatu
  • What → Apa yang sedang dibicarakan atau terjadi
  • Where → Di mana aktivitas berlangsung
Meskipun tidak selalu eksplisit, kamu harus memahami konteks dan *clue* kata-kata dalam percakapan.

Example 1:

Woman: Charlie has the potential to be a professional musician, but he is too lazy to practice.
Man: What a shame!
Narrator: What does the woman say about Charlie?
Answer: C. He is very talented, but he’ll never be professional musician because he does not practice.

Example 2:

Man: This cake is delicious. Who made it?
Woman: My grandmother did. It's her secret recipe.
Narrator: Who made the cake?
Answer: B. The woman’s grandmother

Exercise 2.1

Dengarkan percakapan, lalu tentukan siapa/apa/dimana yang dimaksud dalam konteksnya.

2.b. Who and What in Passive Sentences

Pertanyaan dalam bentuk *passive voice* bisa membuat bingung jika tidak terbiasa. Kamu harus bisa membedakan antara:

  • Active: The manager completed the report.
  • Passive: The report was completed by the manager.
Perhatikan siapa yang melakukan dan siapa yang dikenai tindakan.

Example 1:

Woman: Why were you so late in getting home from work?
Man: My boss had me finish all the month-end reports.
Narrator: What does the man mean?
Answer: B. He was told to stay late and accomplish some work.

Example 2:

Man: I didn’t see the cake at the party.
Woman: It was eaten before you arrived.
Narrator: What does the woman mean?
Answer: D. The cake had already been finished by the time he got there.

Exercise 2.2

Tulis ulang kalimat aktif ke bentuk pasif dan sebaliknya. Tentukan siapa pelakunya dan apa objeknya.

C. Review Exercises

Berikut latihan untuk menguji pemahaman kamu terhadap materi:

1. Who/What/Where

Woman: I can’t believe how crowded the library is today.
Man: Everyone's studying for the midterms.
Narrator: Where does this conversation take place?
  • A. In a classroom
  • B. In a dormitory
  • C. In a library
  • D. In a bookstore

Answer: C. In a library

2. Passive Voice

Man: Who left the lights on in the office?
Woman: I’m not sure, but they were already on when I arrived.
Narrator: What does the woman imply?
  • A. She forgot to turn off the lights
  • B. The lights were on before she came
  • C. She turned on the lights
  • D. The man turned them on

Answer: B. The lights were on before she came
